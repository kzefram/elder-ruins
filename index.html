Welcome to Elder Ruins RPG! A game where you can go on a wild adventure into the ruins of The Elder City and fight fantastical beasts. Join us on this quest to kill the king of the dragons, humble adventurer!

On this adventure we are walking through a dense forest when we come upon some ruins. As we are searching this vast expanse of beautiful site, we can see that a lot of people must have lived long ago. An epic battle must have taken place here for all the buildings to be distroyed like this.

We see a pile of supplies and a path. Oh look at the castle in the distance. It looks like the king of the dragons is waiting for us. What will we do?
